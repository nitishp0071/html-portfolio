Top 3 Best Movies or Series According to Nitish

My top 3 Movies of all time

1. Interstellar

This is my best movie of all time. I have watched it many times and each time feels like a different experience. This movie is one of best in genre viz. sci-fi, adventure, thriller and mystery.

2. One Piece Animated series

This is one of the best animated series and the author behind the manga is a true genius. The amount of imagination put behind this story is out of this world.

3. Hunter x Hunter Animated series

This is yet another animated series like one piece but it has different type of vibe to it. This has more intense action scenes which gives literal goosebumps.
